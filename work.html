SpeakX

EdTech / English-learning for Indian consumers. Gurugram, India.

Product Designer Oct 2024 – Oct 2025 · 1 yr 1 mo Gurugram, Haryana · India · Hybrid

Designed core growth and retention surfaces for SpeakX's English-learning product. Owned the full activation funnel, paid checkout, and built the company's first design system — moving the team from one-off Sketch files to a token-based Figma library shared with engineering.

  • Onboarding redesign — Audited a leaky first-run flow where new users dropped before completing the first lesson. Redesigned the interaction model around progressive disclosure and contextual rewards. +6.01% D1 retention, meaningful lift in new-user activation.
  • Premium checkout funnel — Rebuilt the paid upgrade flow end-to-end: reduced steps, added trust cues, clarified pricing hierarchy. +38.8% paid conversion.
  • Exit-intent retention — Designed contextual popups triggered by behavioural signals, not timers. −4% churn, +4% D1 retention.
  • Live Classes experience — Designed a simulated live workshop format to deepen engagement. +3.5% session time, measurable D7 retention lift.
  • Design system (0 → 1) — Built and scaled SpeakX's first design system. Migrated the team from Sketch to Figma, standardised 80+ components, set up token-based theming — cutting engineering handoff time significantly.
