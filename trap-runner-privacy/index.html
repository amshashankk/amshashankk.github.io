Trap Runner — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 26 June 2026 · App ID: com.amshashankk.traprunner

This Privacy Policy explains how the mobile game Trap Runner ("the game", "we", "us") handles information. The short version: Trap Runner does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. There are no accounts, no logins, and no payments.

1. Information we collect

None that leaves your device. Trap Runner does not ask for, collect, or transmit any personally identifiable information. We do not request your name, email, phone number, location, contacts, photos, or any account credentials.

2. Data stored on your device

To make the game playable, Trap Runner saves a small amount of data locally on your device only using the operating system's standard local storage:

  • Your level progress and unlocked levels
  • Your selected character
  • Your settings, such as music and sound-effect toggles

This data never leaves your device, is never sent to us or any third party, and is automatically removed if you uninstall the game.

3. No accounts, no payments

Trap Runner does not require you to create an account or sign in. There are no in-app purchases, subscriptions, or payments of any kind. We therefore never handle payment or billing information.

4. No advertising or analytics

The game currently shows no third-party advertisements and does not use third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising identifiers. If this changes in a future version, we will update this policy and the app's Play Store Data safety disclosure before any such feature ships.

5. Children's privacy

Trap Runner is a casual game suitable for all age groups and contains no violence, gambling, or mature content. Because the game collects no personal data from anyone, it collects no personal data from children. We do not knowingly collect information from users of any age.

6. Permissions

Trap Runner does not request access to sensitive device permissions such as your camera, microphone, location, contacts, or storage of personal files.

7. Changes to this policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, we will revise the "Last updated" date above. Material changes will be reflected here before they take effect in a published version of the game.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact the developer at amshashankk@gmail.com.
